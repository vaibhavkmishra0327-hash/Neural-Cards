## Publishing Strategy

### Frequency
- **Month 1-3:** 2 posts per week (24 posts)
- **Month 4-6:** 1 post per week (12 posts)
- **Month 7+:** 2 posts per month (evergreen updates)

### Content Format
Each blog post should include:
1. **Hook** (first 100 words)
2. **Table of contents** (jump links)
3. **Visual elements** (diagrams, screenshots)
4. **Code examples** (syntax highlighted)
5. **Key takeaways** (summary box)
6. **Related flashcard deck CTA**
7. **FAQ section** (schema markup)
8. **Related posts** (internal links)

### Distribution Channels
1. **Organic Search** (primary)
2. **Reddit** (r/MachineLearning, r/learnmachinelearning)
3. **Twitter** (with visual snippets)
4. **LinkedIn** (professional audience)
5. **Dev.to / Hashnode** (republish with canonical)
6. **Email Newsletter** (for returning users)

### SEO Optimization
- Target keyword in first 100 words
- H2/H3 structure with keyword variations
- Alt text on all images
- Internal links to 3-5 topic pages
- Meta description with CTA
- Schema.org Article markup
